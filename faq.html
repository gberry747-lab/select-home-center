Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions families ask us most, about financing, what's included, land, delivery, and more.

Buying a new home comes with a lot of questions, and you deserve honest answers before you sign anything. Here are the ones we hear most. If you do not see yours, just call us at 912-208-6065. We are always glad to help.

Do you help with financing?

Yes. We work with lending partners who specialize in manufactured homes, including 21st Mortgage and CalCon Mutual, and we help you get pre-qualified and handle the paperwork every step of the way. There is no cost and no obligation to get started. Learn more on our financing page.

Can I get a home with less-than-perfect credit?

Often, yes. Our lending partners work with real families every day, and there are more paths than most people expect. If your credit is not perfect, please do not count yourself out. Give us a call and we will help you explore your options honestly, with no pressure.

What is included with every home?

Every new home from Select Home Center includes delivery and setup within 100 miles of Folkston, an A/C and heat pump, trim-out, steps, and metal skirting, plus a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty. These are part of buying your home, not surprise add-ons. See the full breakdown in our guide on what's included with every home.

How far do you deliver?

Delivery and setup are included within 100 miles of our Folkston, Georgia lot, which covers much of Southeast Georgia and North Florida. See the areas we serve. If you are outside that range, we can still help, just ask.

Do I need to own land first?

It helps, but it is not required to start the conversation. If you own land, we will help you understand what your site needs. If you do not, we can talk through your options together. Either way, there is never any pressure.

Can I put a manufactured home on land I already own?

In most cases, yes. You will want to confirm local zoning and any deed restrictions, make sure the site can be served by water, power, and septic or sewer, and get the proper permit. We help buyers sort this out often. For the full walk-through, read can you put a manufactured home on land you own?

What does the Free Lifetime Warranty cover?

Our exclusive Free Lifetime Home Warranty is included with every home we sell and is designed to give you long-term peace of mind. Visit our Lifetime Warranty page or call us at 912-208-6065 for the full details.

What brands do you carry?

We carry Clayton, its budget-friendly TRU line, and Scotbilt. Together they give us quality homes at several price points, from cozy single-section homes to spacious multi-section family homes.

Single-wide or double-wide, which should I choose?

It depends on your family, your land, and your budget. A single-wide is cozy, efficient, and budget-friendly. A double-wide gives you more room to spread out. Our guide on single-wide vs. double-wide walks through how to decide.

Do you sell to buyers in Florida?

Yes. We are licensed in Georgia and sell from our Folkston lot, then arrange delivery and setup for Florida buyers, similar to buying a vehicle from a dealer just across the state line. Families in Nassau County and across North Florida buy from us regularly.

Why do your homes say "Call for Price"?

Because an honest quote is built around the exact home you choose, whether you have land, how you plan to finance, and what is included. We would rather give you a complete, accurate number for your situation than an eye-catching price that grows once the add-ons appear. The quote is always free. Read more in what does a manufactured home really cost?

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes. Se Habla Español. We are glad to help Spanish-speaking families through every step of buying a new home.

Still have a question?

Stop by our Folkston lot or give us a call. We are happy to walk you through anything and help you find the home that fits your family and your budget.
