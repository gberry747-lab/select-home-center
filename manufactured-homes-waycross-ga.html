New Manufactured Homes for Waycross, GA

A short drive from Waycross and Ware County to our Folkston lot, where honest pricing and a free lifetime warranty come standard on every new home.

Serving Waycross, GA

If you live in Waycross or anywhere across Ware County and you are ready for a brand-new manufactured home, Select Home Center is worth the trip. Our lot sits in Folkston, about a 30 mile drive southeast, and families make that short run all the time because of what they find when they get here: real homes, straight answers, and pricing you can actually plan around.

Waycross has always been a hard-working town, from the rail yards to the businesses lining Plant Avenue, and the folks who call Ware County home deserve a place to shop for a new home that treats them the same way they treat their own neighbors. That is how we try to run things. We opened in early 2024 with local families in mind, and we would rather earn your trust with clear answers than push you toward a sale. Come out, walk the homes, ask every question you can think of, and take your time.

The drive from Waycross runs you down through Charlton County to Folkston, roughly 30 miles depending on where in Ware County you start. It is an easy trip on a Saturday morning, and it is a whole lot shorter than settling for less home than your family needs. Once you are here, you can see the layouts in person, picture your furniture in the rooms, and get a feel for the fit and finish that photos never quite capture.

Clayton and Scotbilt Homes for Ware County Families

We hand-pick our homes from two of the most trusted names in the business, Clayton and Scotbilt. Clayton also builds its budget-friendly TRU line, which is a great fit for buyers who want a solid, modern home while keeping the payment comfortable. Between single-wide and double-wide layouts, there is room here for a first home, a growing family, or a place to retire to on a little land outside town.

Whatever you pick, we set it up right and we do not nickel-and-dime you on the things that ought to come standard. Here is what is included on every new home.

Included with every new home: Delivery and setup within 100 miles, A/C and heat pump, trim-out, steps, and metal skirting, plus a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty that covers your kitchen appliances, plumbing and water heater, electrical, and the A/C and heat.

Financing Made Simple

Buying a home should not feel like a maze. We work with two experienced lenders, 21st Mortgage and CalCon Mutual, who understand manufactured-home buyers and know this part of Georgia. Tell us what you are comfortable spending each month, and we will help match you with a home and a path that fits. You are welcome to ask us anything about the process before you ever fill out a form. Our financing page walks through the basics, and you can always call or text us at 912-208-6065 to talk it through.

The Free Lifetime Home Warranty

This is the part that sets us apart, and it costs you nothing extra. Every new home from Select Home Center comes with a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty. That means the big-ticket systems, your kitchen appliances, plumbing and water heater, electrical, and the A/C and heat, are covered for as long as you own the home. It is real peace of mind for a family that plans to put down roots, and it is one more reason the drive from Waycross is worth making. You can read the full details on our lifetime warranty page.
