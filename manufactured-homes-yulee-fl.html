New Manufactured Homes for Yulee, FL

About 35 miles from our Folkston lot. New Clayton, TRU, and Scotbilt homes, honest pricing, and a free lifetime warranty on every one.

Serving Yulee, FL

Yulee has grown faster than just about anywhere in Nassau County, and with that growth came prices that have pushed a lot of local families to look harder at their options. Select Home Center is about 35 miles northwest in Folkston, and we see plenty of Yulee buyers who want a brand-new home without a coastal price attached to it.

Being this close to Amelia Island and Fernandina Beach is wonderful right up until you shop for a house. A new manufactured home on your own land is one of the few ways left to stay in the area, get something genuinely new, and keep the payment somewhere reasonable. We help people do that regularly, whether the land is off SR-200, back toward the county line, or a family lot somebody has been holding onto.

The drive up to Folkston is an easy one, and what you get for it is a small family-owned lot instead of a chain. You deal with the people who run the business. We will be straight with you about what things cost, show you homes that fit your situation instead of the ones with the best margin, and let you take your time.

Clayton and Scotbilt homes built for this climate

We stock Clayton and Scotbilt. Clayton is among the most trusted builders in the country, and its TRU line exists for buyers who want new construction at a payment that works. Scotbilt is Georgia-built and put together for the same heat, humidity, and storm season you deal with on the Nassau County coast. You will find single-wide and double-wide plans and layouts with real kitchens and real storage. Take a look at our inventory page before you come.

Every new home from Select Home Center includes: delivery and setup within 100 miles, which covers Yulee and the Nassau County coast, plus a central A/C and heat pump, full trim-out, steps, metal skirting, and our FREE Lifetime Home Warranty.

Delivery and setup down to Yulee

Yulee is inside our delivery range, and we handle the haul and the setup. Call us and we will confirm delivery for your address and walk through what your county asks for on permits and site preparation, so the timeline is clear before you commit to a home.

Financing and the free lifetime warranty
