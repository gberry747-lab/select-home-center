New Manufactured Homes for St. Marys, GA

Coastal Camden County families shop our Folkston lot for Clayton and Scotbilt homes, honest pricing, and a free lifetime warranty on every new home.

Serving St. Marys, GA

If you live in St. Marys or anywhere in coastal Camden County and you are thinking about a new manufactured home, Select Home Center is your nearby dealer. Our lot sits about a 30 mile drive inland in Folkston, and families from all along the coast make the trip because the selection, the pricing, and the warranty are worth it.

St. Marys is a beautiful place to call home, from the historic waterfront to the quiet neighborhoods that stretch back toward Kingsland and the county line. Plenty of local families would rather put down roots on their own land here than keep renting, and a new manufactured home is one of the most affordable ways to do exactly that. We work with folks who have land near the river, out toward the marsh, or on a family lot that has been in the family for years, and we help them find a home that fits both the property and the budget.

The drive from St. Marys up to our Folkston lot runs roughly 30 miles, a straightforward trip inland along the highways you already know. It is an easy morning out, and it is the reason so many coastal buyers choose us over dealers that are farther away or that push harder on price. When you walk our lot you get straight answers, a real look at the homes, and time to think it over with no pressure at all.

Every home we carry comes from Clayton or Scotbilt, two of the most trusted names in the business. Clayton also builds the budget-friendly TRU line, which is a great fit if you want a brand-new home while keeping the payment comfortable. Scotbilt is known for solid, well-built homes that hold up beautifully in our Southeast Georgia climate. Whatever you are picturing, we will walk you through the floor plans and help you match one to your land and your family.

Every new home includes: delivery and setup within 100 miles, an A/C and heat pump, trim-out, steps, metal skirting, and a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty. There are no surprise add-ons hiding in the fine print, just an honest package built to get you settled.

Financing your St. Marys home

You do not have to figure out financing on your own. We work with two well-established lenders, 21st Mortgage and CalCon Mutual, who understand manufactured-home loans and coastal Georgia buyers. Bring us a sense of what you can comfortably spend each month and we will help you explore your options with them. We do not post prices or rates online because every situation is different, so the honest answer is a real conversation, not a number on a screen. See our financing page for more on how it works, then reach out and we will point you in the right direction.

Your free lifetime warranty

The warranty is the part St. Marys buyers tell us they appreciate most. Every new home from Select Home Center includes a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty that covers the kitchen appliances, the plumbing and water heater, the electrical, and the A/C and heat. It is our way of standing behind the home long after we hand you the keys, so you can settle in near the coast with real peace of mind. Learn what it covers on our lifetime warranty page.
