New Manufactured Homes for Callahan, FL

Roughly 30 miles north of Callahan on US-1. New Clayton, TRU, and Scotbilt homes, honest pricing, and a free lifetime warranty on every one.

Serving Callahan, FL

Callahan sits at the crossroads of Nassau County, and our lot in Folkston is about 30 miles north of it. That is a half-hour run up US-1, and it is a trip a steady stream of Callahan families make when they are ready to put a brand-new home on their own land.

Callahan has held onto its acreage while Jacksonville has crept closer, and that combination is what makes a manufactured home make so much sense here. Land that would be unaffordable twenty miles south is still within reach, and a new home on it costs a fraction of building from scratch. We work with people doing exactly that all the time, whether the parcel is out toward Bryceville, up along US-301, or on land that has been in the family for years.

The reason folks drive past closer lots is not complicated. We are a small family-owned business, so the person showing you the home is the person who runs the place. Nobody is working you toward a number. We will look at your budget honestly, tell you what fits and what does not, and give you the time to think it over.

Clayton, TRU, and Scotbilt on the lot

Our inventory comes from Clayton and Scotbilt, two names that have earned their reputations. Clayton builds at a scale nobody else matches, and its TRU line is aimed squarely at buyers who want a new home at a comfortable payment. Scotbilt is built in Georgia for a climate identical to yours. Between them you get single-wide and double-wide plans with the kind of kitchens and layouts people actually want. Browse our inventory page and then come walk through them.

Every new home from Select Home Center includes: delivery and setup within 100 miles, which takes in Callahan and the rest of Nassau County, plus a central A/C and heat pump, full trim-out, steps, metal skirting, and our FREE Lifetime Home Warranty.

Getting the home to your land

Callahan is well inside our delivery range, and the haul down US-1 is a familiar one for us. Call and we will confirm delivery and setup for your specific address, and talk you through what your county expects on permitting and getting the site ready. Better to sort that out early than to find it at the end.

Financing and the free lifetime warranty

We work with 21st Mortgage and CalCon Mutual, both of whom handle manufactured-home lending as their main business and work with Florida buyers regularly. Our financing page covers the basics. Every new home also comes with a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty on the kitchen appliances, the plumbing and water heater, the electrical, and the A/C and heat, spelled out on our lifetime warranty page.
