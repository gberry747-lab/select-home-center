Serving Kingsland, GA

If you live in Kingsland or anywhere in Camden County and you are ready for a brand-new manufactured home, we would love to meet you. Select Home Center sits about 25 miles northwest in Folkston, an easy drive that plenty of Kingsland families make when they want a real person to walk them through the whole process.

Kingsland has grown fast, and so has the cost of a stick-built house. A new manufactured home is how a lot of local working families get the space, the modern kitchen, and the low-maintenance living they want without a payment that keeps them up at night. Whether you are putting a home on your own land out toward Tarboro or St. Marys, or moving up from a rental in town, we can help you figure out what actually fits your budget and your plans.

Why do folks from Camden County keep making the trip to Folkston? Because we keep it simple and we keep it honest. We are a small family-owned lot, not a high-pressure chain, so when you visit you deal with the people who actually run the place. We take the time to learn what you need, show you homes that make sense for your situation, and give you straight answers on price. No games, no rushing you toward a sale.

Clayton and Scotbilt homes worth the drive

We hand-pick our inventory from two of the most trusted names in the business. Clayton is one of the largest and most respected homebuilders in the country, and its budget-friendly TRU line is a favorite for buyers who want a great new home at a lower price point. Scotbilt is a Georgia-built favorite known for solid construction and floor plans that just live well. Between the two brands you will find single-wide and double-wide layouts, roomy kitchens, and plenty of ways to make the home feel like yours. Take a look at what is on the lot on our inventory page, then come see it in person.

Every new home from Select Home Center includes: delivery and setup within 100 miles (which covers Kingsland and all of Camden County), a central A/C and heat pump, full trim-out, steps, metal skirting, and our FREE Lifetime Home Warranty. It is all built into the deal, not tacked on at the end.

Financing your Kingsland home

Worried about how it all comes together? You are not alone, and it is easier than most people expect. We work with trusted lending partners including 21st Mortgage and CalCon Mutual, who specialize in manufactured-home loans and understand buyers here in Southeast Georgia. Tell us a little about your situation and we will help you start the conversation with the right people. You can learn more on our financing page, and there is no pressure to commit to anything just by asking questions.

The free lifetime warranty

This is the part we are proudest of. Every new home we sell comes with a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty that covers your kitchen appliances, your plumbing and water heater, your electrical, and your A/C and heat. It is our way of standing behind the homes long after you have the keys, so you can settle into your Kingsland home with real peace of mind. The full details are on our lifetime warranty page.
