New Manufactured Homes for Hilliard, FL

About 15 miles up US-1 and you are at our lot. New Clayton, TRU, and Scotbilt homes, honest pricing, and a free lifetime warranty on every one.

Serving Hilliard, FL

Hilliard is the closest Florida town to our lot, roughly 15 miles south of us on US-1. That makes Select Home Center the nearest new manufactured home dealer for a lot of Nassau County families, and the trip up is short enough that people often come look on a weekday afternoon.

Northern Nassau County still has room to breathe, and that is exactly why people want to build here. Plenty of Hilliard buyers already own an acre or two, or have family land they can put a home on, and a new manufactured home is the most direct way to turn that land into a place to live. You skip the years-long wait and the stick-built price, and you still get a new kitchen, new everything, and a warranty behind it.

Crossing a state line to shop sounds like a complication until you do it. Folkston is a straight run up US-1, closer than most of the Jacksonville-area lots, and you are dealing with a small family-owned business instead of a sales floor with quotas. We will talk through your land, your budget, and what you actually want out of the home, and we will give you real answers on price.

Clayton and Scotbilt homes worth the short drive

We hand-pick our inventory from Clayton and Scotbilt. Clayton is one of the most established builders in the country, and its TRU line keeps the payment down for buyers who want new without stretching. Scotbilt is Georgia-built and holds up beautifully in the same heat and storms you get in Nassau County. You will find single-wide and double-wide layouts and plans that live a lot bigger than the square footage suggests. Start with our inventory page and then come see them.

Every new home from Select Home Center includes: delivery and setup within 100 miles, which covers Hilliard and Nassau County comfortably, plus a central A/C and heat pump, full trim-out, steps, metal skirting, and our FREE Lifetime Home Warranty.

Delivery and setup into Nassau County

Hilliard sits comfortably inside our delivery range, and getting a home down US-1 to you is about as straightforward as it gets. Give us a call and we will confirm delivery for your exact address and walk you through what your county asks for on permits and site prep, so there are no surprises once you have picked a home.
