Areas We Serve

Families drive to our Folkston lot from all across Southeast Georgia and Northeast Florida.

Select Home Center sits right in Folkston, Georgia, and we happily welcome buyers from all across the region. Wherever you are calling home, you are welcome on our lot, and plenty of families tell us the short drive was more than worth it once they saw everything for themselves.

What makes the trip worthwhile is simple: a real selection of new Clayton, TRU, and Scotbilt homes to walk through, honest pricing with zero pressure, and a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty on every new home we sell. Come see the homes in person, ask us anything, and let us help you find the one that fits your family and your budget.
