About Select Home Center

Affordable, Stylish, Yours. Get to know the local team behind your next new manufactured home in Folkston, Georgia.

Right here in Folkston, Georgia, Select Home Center has become the place local families turn to when it's time for a new manufactured home. We believe a top-of-the-line home should still come with honest pricing and zero pressure, so we take the time to learn what you need and your budget, then help you find the home that fits you perfectly.

We opened our doors in early 2024 with hard-working families in mind, the kind of folks who deserve a beautiful, modern home without a payment that keeps them up at night. From the first time you walk the lot to the day we hand over the keys, our team sweats the details so the whole process stays refreshingly easy. Tell us what you're picturing, and we'll help build it around your budget and your plans.

What We Stand For

Honesty. Quality. Satisfaction.

Honesty

It all starts with straight talk. We'd rather earn your trust with clear answers and zero pressure than rush you toward a sale. A home is too big a decision for anything less.

Quality

We hand-pick our homes from two of the most trusted names in the industry, Clayton and Scotbilt (including Clayton's budget-friendly TRU line), and we personally stand behind every setup from delivery to the final walk-through.

Satisfaction

Your happiness is the entire point. We take the time to understand what you need and what you can spend, so the road to owning your home feels smooth, supported, and genuinely yours.

Every new home includes: Delivery & setup within 100 miles · A/C & heat pump · Trim-out · Steps · Metal skirting · FREE Lifetime Home Warranty
