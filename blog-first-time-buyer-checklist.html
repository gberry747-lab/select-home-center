The First-Time Buyer's Step-by-Step Checklist

New year, new home. Here is the whole journey laid out in simple, doable steps.

Buyer's Guide

Buying your first home is exciting, and if you have never done it before, it can feel like a lot to keep straight. It really is not, once you see it as a series of small steps. Here is the whole journey from first thought to holding your keys, laid out plainly. Print it, check off each step, and lean on us at any point along the way.

1. Set a comfortable budget

Start with what feels right for your family every month, not just the sticker price. Knowing your comfortable monthly number keeps the whole search grounded. If you are not sure where to start, we are happy to talk it through with no pressure.

2. Get pre-qualified

Before you fall in love with a specific home, it helps to know roughly what you can borrow. Getting pre-qualified with a lender gives you that number so you can shop with confidence. We work with lenders who specialize in manufactured homes and welcome a wide range of credit situations. See our guide on how financing works.

3. Decide where the home will go

Your home needs a place to live too. If you already own land, wonderful. If not, that becomes part of your plan. Either way, think about the town, the lot, and what the site will need. Our guide on putting a home on land you own walks through the basics.

4. Choose your home

Now for the fun part. Walk our current inventory, compare floor plans from Clayton, its budget-friendly TRU line, and Scotbilt, and picture your family living there. Not sure how much space you need? Our guide on single-wide vs. double-wide can help you decide.

5. Prepare your land

If you are placing the home on your own property, the site needs clearing, access, utilities, and a foundation. We cover this in detail in how to prepare your land. We help you sequence it so nothing holds up your timeline.

6. Finalize financing and paperwork

With a home chosen and a lender on board, we help coordinate the paperwork and keep everyone on the same page. This is where all the earlier steps pay off, because you are working from real numbers, not guesses.

7. Delivery, setup, and move-in

The last step is the best one. Every new home from us includes delivery and setup within 100 miles, an A/C and heat pump, trim-out, steps, and metal skirting, plus a FREE Lifetime Home Warranty. For a look at that day, see what to expect on delivery and setup day. Then you are home.

You do not have to memorize any of this. Keep this checklist handy, take the steps one at a time, and call us whenever something feels unclear. Helping first-time buyers become homeowners is the whole reason we are here.
