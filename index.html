Welcome to Select Home Center

Your Trusted Manufactured Home Dealer in Folkston, GA

Select Home Center is your ultimate destination for all your manufactured home needs. We are a family-owned, locally operated dealer here in Folkston, Georgia, and since opening our doors in early 2024 we have helped families across Southeast Georgia and Northeast Florida find a quality new home they can be proud of.

We believe buying a home should be refreshingly simple: honest pricing, no pressure, and real help from your first question to move-in day. Every new home comes with our FREE Lifetime Home Warranty, plus delivery, setup, A/C, steps, and skirting included. Come see us in Folkston, or call 912-208-6065 and we will help you find the home that fits your family and your budget.
